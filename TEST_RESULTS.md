## Known Issue (Resolved)

**Problem:** Calibre 7.0.0 caused `merge_and_build` to time out (ebook-convert hung on PDF/EPUB generation via subprocess).
**Fix:** Upgrade to Calibre ≥ 9.x — `winget upgrade calibre.calibre` (tested on 9.11.0).
**Verification:** Full pipeline (111 chunks, 589KB text) completes in <3 minutes on 9.11.0.
